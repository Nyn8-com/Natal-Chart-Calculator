Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Natal Charts and our calculator

What is a Natal Chart?

+

A natal chart, also known as a birth chart, is a snapshot of the sky at the exact moment and place you were born. It maps the positions of planets, stars, and other celestial bodies, creating a unique cosmic fingerprint that reveals your personality, life path, and potential.

How accurate is the birth time for calculations?

+

Birth time accuracy is crucial for precise natal chart calculations, especially for determining your Ascendant (Rising sign) and house positions. The more accurate your birth time, the more precise your chart will be. Even a few minutes difference can significantly impact your chart interpretation.

What do the different houses represent?

+

The 12 houses in astrology represent different areas of life: 1st (self, appearance), 2nd (money, values), 3rd (communication, siblings), 4th (home, family), 5th (creativity, romance), 6th (work, health), 7th (partnerships), 8th (transformation, shared resources), 9th (higher learning, travel), 10th (career, reputation), 11th (friends, groups), and 12th (spirituality, subconscious).

Can I use this calculator if I don't know my exact birth time?

+

While you can still get valuable insights about your Sun, Moon, and planetary positions without an exact birth time, your Ascendant and house positions may not be accurate. For the most comprehensive reading, try to get your birth time from birth records or ask family members.

How often should I check my natal chart?

+

Your natal chart remains the same throughout your life as it's based on your birth moment. However, you can refer to it regularly for self-reflection and understanding. For ongoing guidance, look into transits (current planetary movements) and progressions (evolving aspects of your chart) which change over time.
